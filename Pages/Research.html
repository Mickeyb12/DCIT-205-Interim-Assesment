    • Research Overview

    Here in University of Ghana, we take on significant contemporary challenges with the aim of making
    a positive impact on the world. Our research covers a wide range of topics, from
    creating advanced robotics to exploring the origins of the universe, cutting
    across different disciplines and generating innovative ideas.

    Libraries

    Ranked the No. 1 public research university
    library in West Africa, the UG library
    system includes three main libraries, 18 subject-
    specialty libraries, and 11 affiliated libraries with
    special collections. The holdings include more
    than 1 million book volumes.
